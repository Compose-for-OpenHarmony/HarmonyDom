# Changelog
## [0.1.4] 2024-03-25
New:
- Support `List`
## [0.1.3] 2024-02-09
New:
- Support `Image`
- Support attributes for `Text`:
  * `fontSize`
  * `fontColor`
  * `maxLines`
  * `spans`
## [0.1.2] 2024-01-27
New:
- Support `Column` and `Row`
- Support common attributes:
  * `width`
  * `height`
  * `padding`
  * `layoutWeight`
## [0.1.1] 2024-01-01
New:
- Support `FontStyle` for `TextNode`
## [0.1.0] 2024-01-01
Initial release.
